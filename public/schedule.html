Timing verification

  • Doors open at 4:00 PM. Earliest guests can enter the building.
  • All Showing 1 movies start at 4:30 PM (5-min DEF intro, then feature). One call time for the whole house, simpler floor-ops than a staggered start.
  • Movies end 6:13 → 6:47 PM. Dragon at 6:13, Breadwinner at 6:15, Paddington 2 at 6:19, Mandalorian (longest) at 6:47.
  • Auction closes 6:30 PM. Falls inside the spillover window — guests in shorter movies are out by then; Mandalorian audience is the IMAX premier crowd.
  • Mandalorian S1 → S2 turnaround is tight: 28 minutes (6:47 PM end → 7:15 PM lights). 15 min crew turnover finishes 7:02 PM, leaving 13 min for dinner serve and walk-in. Confirmed acceptable per Kara — last year's floor was 5:00 / 7:50 and this matches it.
  • Showing 2 dinner doors open 6:45 PM for the three shorter-movie auds; Mandalorian IMAX doors as soon as crew clears (~7:02 PM).
  • All Showing 2 movies start at 7:15 PM. Dinner serve + walk-in time is the binding constraint, not crew turnover.
  • Showing 2 movies end 8:58 → 9:32 PM. Dragon out first at 8:58, Mandalorian out last at 9:32. House clear by 10 PM.
  • Total bookable inventory: 1,883 seats across two sessions (954 S1 + 929 S2).
