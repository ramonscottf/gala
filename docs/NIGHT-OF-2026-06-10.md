## Walk-up flow (door, no reservation)
1. Seat Mover v2 → right room → tap open seat(s) → **Assign walk-up →**
2. Name (required) + phone and/or email + dinner → Assign.
3. They instantly get texted/emailed tickets w/ seat-map link + QR. No contact? Success panel shows the link to read them / QR to show.
   - House sponsor: `DEF Gala Walk-ups` (id 116). Collision on a just-taken seat = clean 409, nothing half-created.
